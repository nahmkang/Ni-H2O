Ni-H₂O Pourbaix diagram (25 °C)

The simplest Ni system with no complexing agents. Ni²⁺ is the only dissolved species; Ni(OH)₂ and Ni(OH)₃ are the solid phases. As Ni concentration rises, the dissolved-Ni²⁺ region expands and Ni(OH)₂ precipitation pH shifts left.

0.1 M
All Nernst equations used in this diagram

S = 0.05916 V (which is RT·ln10/F at 25 °C). Solids have activity = 1. Below the precipitation pH, [Ni²⁺] = C_Ni. Above it, [Ni²⁺] is pinned by Ksp.
